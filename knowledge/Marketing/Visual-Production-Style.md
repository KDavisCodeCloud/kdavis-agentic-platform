## When to use this style
- Teaching/educational posts (frameworks, mental models, process maps)
- "How I built X" posts that benefit from a hand-drawn, approachable feel
- Repurposed concept graphics in Decoded brand style
- Anything where sketch = credibility over polish

## When NOT to use this style
- Data visualizations — use Canva infographic template
- Product marketing assets — use Canva brand kit
- Anything that needs to look enterprise / polished / production-grade
- DecodedSix visuals — those use the Vice City design system, not hand-drawn
