---

### Phase 1 — Pre-launch (now → Nov 19 2027)

#### Content (DSX-CA1 agent)
- 3 articles/week: news / evergreen / conversion
- 48+ articles indexed and interlinked BEFORE Nov 19
- Every article: SEO + AEO + GEO + SXO standards, schema, E-E-A-T, 1200+ words
- **CREDIBILITY RULE:** every claim tagged `CONFIRMED` / `LEAKED (unverified)` / `SPECULATION`
- Honesty is the differentiator AND the E-E-A-T signal in a sea of slop

#### Newsletter capture (build at launch)
- Signup on every article page + site header
- Lead magnet: "GTA 6 Confirmed vs Rumored — the sourced master list" (updated weekly)
- Weekly digest: confirmed news vs debunked rumors
- Cadence: weekly, MKT-N1 Newsletter Agent (DecodedSix variant)
- This list survives any algorithm change = owned asset

#### Community seeding (HUMAN-POSTED, agent-assisted only)
- Communities: r/GTA6, r/GTAVI, GTAForums, relevant Discords
- Agent DRAFTS suggested responses — you or wife POST personally
- 90% genuine participation / 10% relevant link-drop
- NEVER cross-post same link · NEVER present speculation as leak · NEVER automate posting (ban risk)
- Anti-slop: original analysis / organized sourced info only, never repetition

#### Technical / distribution
- AI crawlers unblocked: `GPTBot`, `ClaudeBot`, `PerplexityBot`, `Google-Extended` in robots.txt
- Affiliate programs live BEFORE first conversion article: Amazon Associates, Fanatical, CDKeys, Green Man Gaming
- Fast Core Web Vitals (SXO) — pages hold visitors, feeds ranking
- Long-tail keywords first (rank in weeks, build authority)

## PART 2 — MSE COMPLETE STRATEGY

### Locked decisions
- Product model: **PAID-ONLY**, low price point — no free tier, no trial
- Cold start: COMMUNITY + APOLLO led (dual primary)
- Search: SEO/AEO/GEO/SXO built INTO the product/site from day 1
- NO launch-platform dependency (Product Hunt / AppSumo not needed for paid-only)
- Campaign: MKT-ORCH fires on Verdict approval

### Why paid-only changes the funnel
- Paid-only = quality over volume. Need dozens of RIGHT buyers, not thousands of free signups.
- No tire-kickers, no free-tier support burden, revenue from customer #1.
- Product Hunt / AppSumo suit freemium / free-trial (volume spikes) — not this model.
- Motion: reach qualified buyers → prove value fast → paid conversion.

---

### The cold-start motion (first 100 paying users per product)

#### Primary 1 — Community-led
- MKT-C1 Community Scout identifies where THIS product's ICP lives (Reddit, FB groups, Discord, niche forums — per product)
- Same credibility rules as DecodedSix: human-posted, value-first, no spam
- Agent drafts, human posts
- Goal: become known in the community BEFORE launch, launch to warm audience

#### Primary 2 — Apollo-led (outreach)
- MKT-O1 builds Apollo list from product ICP (out of research report)
- MKT-O2 writes 2-touch warm DM in ICP's exact pain language
- Trigger event filter (not just title) = buying signal
- HITL before any send · MKT-10 compliance guard

#### Built-in — Search (the product carries its own SEO)
- Product site has SEO/AEO/GEO/SXO from launch
- MKT-S1 SEO factory produces problem-aware content per niche
- Long-tail first, credibility-dense, AI-crawler-open
- Compounds in background while community + Apollo drive early users

---

### The orchestrator flow (confirmed)
```
Verdict approves
  → MKT-ORCH reads ICP + research report
  → fires: Apollo list + DM sequence + email sequence (systeme.io) + SEO factory
  → channel selection based on where ICP lives
  → all drafts to HITL
  → approved → campaign live
```

---

### Pricing / packaging (locked to the model)
- Decided per-product at Verdict (see [[MSE-Pricing-Framework]])
- Low price = lower friction for paid-only (impulse-affordable)
- No packaging tiers needed early — one price, one product

### Email (paid-only nuance)
- No free trial = email nurtures toward PURCHASE, not trial-to-paid conversion
- Sequence: problem awareness → product as solution → proof → offer
- MKT-O3 loads into systeme.io on approval, per product

### First-revenue assumption (honest)
- Paid-only + low price = faster to first dollar (no trial delay)
- Community + Apollo = warm first users, not cold SEO wait
- First customer: within ~2 weeks of launch (warm channels ready)
- $4K floor: 60–90 days of growth (Verdict-validated demand + stacking)
