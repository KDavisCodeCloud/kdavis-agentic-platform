## How to fill (monthly batch — 30-45 min)

Answer these three categories:

**1. How I did it** — what you built or figured out that others in your lane would want to know
- Be specific: what problem, what decision, what the result was
- "How I did it" not "how to" — your specific experience is the moat

**2. Conviction posts** — what you believe about [agentic systems / 1-person biz / AI era] that most people get wrong
- One strong opinion per post
- State it directly, explain why, back it with a story

**3. Journey/build-in-public** — what happened this month worth sharing
- Wins, failures, decisions made, unexpected lessons
- Cloud/AI expertise + a fully human builder's life (gardening, faith, family, motivation) shaping the business = the angle no competitor can copy. USAF service is part of the journey — mention it when it's genuinely relevant, don't lead with it.
